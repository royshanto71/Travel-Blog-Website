About Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is a popular tourist destination in Bangladesh, famous for its natural sea beach, which is the longest unbroken sandy sea beach in the world. The town is bustling with tourists year-round, offering stunning views of the Bay of Bengal.

Things to Do

  • Relax on the beach and enjoy the sunset
  • Visit Himchari National Park
  • Explore local seafood restaurants
  • Take a boat ride to Maheshkhali Island
