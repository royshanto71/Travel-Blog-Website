Sylhet Trip

Exploring the Beauty of Sylhet

Day 1: Arrival in Sylhet

We arrived in Sylhet early in the morning. The weather was pleasant and the city was bustling with life. After checking into our hotel, we enjoyed a traditional breakfast.

Day 2: Exploring Ratargul Swamp Forest

Our second day was dedicated to visiting the famous Ratargul Swamp Forest. The boat ride through the forest was serene and breathtaking.

Day 3: Jaflong Adventure

On the third day, we traveled to Jaflong, known for its rolling hills and tea gardens. The view of the river and the mountains was unforgettable.
